# Aidly Ads Strategy - Post LinkedIn Analysis

## Current Situation (LinkedIn Ads Performance)

**Campaign Period:** Dec 26, 2025 - Jan 24, 2026 (30 days)

**Results:**
- **Total Spent:** €167.93
- **Clicks:** 11
- **Impressions:** 4,882
- **Average CPC:** €15.27
- **CTR:** 0.225%
- **Conversions:** 0
- **Cost per Conversion:** Infinite (no conversions)

**Analysis:**

❌ **Critical Issues:**
1. **Zero conversions** - Not a single trial signup from €168 spent
2. **Extremely high CPC** - €15.27 is 5-10× too expensive for B2B SaaS cold traffic
3. **Low CTR** - 0.225% vs LinkedIn average of 0.5-1%
4. **Wrong audience** - LinkedIn B2B audience doesn't match Shopify merchant profile
5. **Small sample size** - Only 11 clicks isn't enough to test conversion rate properly

**Why LinkedIn Failed:**

1. **Audience Mismatch:**
   - LinkedIn targets: Corporate employees, enterprise IT, SaaS professionals
   - Your ideal customer: Shopify store owners (e-commerce entrepreneurs)
   - Shopify merchants don't actively browse LinkedIn for tools

2. **High Competition:**
   - B2B SaaS ads on LinkedIn are extremely competitive
   - Enterprise software companies bid €20-50 per click
   - You're competing with Salesforce, Zendesk, HubSpot budgets

3. **Wrong Intent:**
   - LinkedIn users browse for networking/content, not shopping for tools
   - Low commercial intent = low conversion rate
   - Even if you got clicks, they're unlikely to convert

4. **Landing Page Issue:**
   - The bug we just fixed may have hurt conversions
   - But with 0 conversions from 11 clicks, there's a bigger problem

**Verdict:** ❌ PAUSE ALL LINKEDIN ADS IMMEDIATELY

## Recommended Ads Strategy (Prioritized)

### TIER 1: Google Ads - Shopify Keywords (START HERE)

**Why Google Ads for Shopify:**
- ✅ **High intent** - People actively searching "shopify customer support software"
- ✅ **Better targeting** - Can target Shopify-specific keywords
- ✅ **Lower CPC** - €2-6 per click (vs €15 on LinkedIn)
- ✅ **Proven channel** - Competitors (Gorgias, Re:amaze) succeed here
- ✅ **Direct ROI measurement** - Clear path: search → click → trial → paid

**Campaign Structure:**

**Keywords:**
```

Description 1: AI drafts responses using your Shopify order data. 2-minute setup. Try 5 emails free.
```

**Expected Performance:**
- Impressions: 1,500-2,500/month

**Break-Even CPA:**
