## 💡 Pro Tips for Maximum SEO Impact

### Content Writing:
1. **Write from experience** - Share real insights
2. **Include examples** - Screenshots, case studies
3. **Add internal links** - Link to pricing, other posts
4. **Clear CTAs** - "Try Aidly Free" at end of each post
5. **Use keywords naturally** - Don't force them

### Link Building:
1. **Share everywhere** - LinkedIn, Twitter, Reddit (carefully)
2. **Answer Quora** - Real help, mention Aidly naturally
3. **Guest posting** - Reach out to SaaS/ecommerce blogs
4. **Directory listings** - Product Hunt, G2, Capterra
5. **Customer stories** - Turn testimonials into case studies
